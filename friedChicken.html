Fried Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4-6 pieces of chicken (any combination of legs, thighs, or wings)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together the flour, salt, paprika, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the milk and egg.
  4. Dip each piece of chicken into the milk and egg mixture, then coat it in the flour mixture.
  5. Heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Add the chicken pieces to the hot oil, being careful not to overcrowd the pan.
  7. Fry the chicken for 5-7 minutes per side or until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Transfer the chicken to a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until cooked through.
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving.
